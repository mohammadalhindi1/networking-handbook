The roadmap is a public progress contract. A chapter is marked complete only when it follows the shared template, has working navigation, includes a practical exercise, and passes an editorial and technical review.

## Definition of done

A chapter is complete when it has:

- all mandatory sections from the chapter template;
- technically accurate terminology and packet flow;
- at least one useful Mermaid diagram or maintained visual;
- Linux commands with purpose, example, and output interpretation;
- a Wireshark display filter and field walkthrough;
- troubleshooting steps based on observable evidence;
- interview questions and a mixed quiz with answers;
- working previous, home, and next navigation links;
- no secrets, personal addresses, or sensitive data in examples or captures.

## Release strategy

- **v0.1 — Foundations:** chapters 00–03 and initial labs.
- **v0.2 — Addressing:** chapters 04–08 and addressing labs.
- **v0.3 — Delivery:** chapters 09–14 and transport/service captures.
- **v0.4 — Infrastructure:** chapters 15–21 and troubleshooting playbooks.
- **v1.0 — Complete handbook:** career and platform tracks, editorial review, and stable navigation.
